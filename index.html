    Supported Formats

    Time Converter automatically detects and converts a wide range of timestamp formats:

    • Unix timestamps — seconds (e.g. 1700000000) and milliseconds (e.g. 1700000000000)
    • ISO 8601 — e.g. 2024-01-15T09:30:00Z
    • RFC 2822 — e.g. Mon, 15 Jan 2024 09:30:00 +0000
    • SQL datetime — e.g. 2024-01-15 09:30:00
    • Slash dates — e.g. 01/15/2024 with US/EU toggle
    • Relative expressions — e.g. 2 hours ago, yesterday, next friday

    Features

    • Auto-detection — paste any timestamp and the format is identified instantly
    • Timezone conversion — convert between 400+ timezones with searchable dropdown
    • One-click copy — copy any output format to clipboard with a single click
    • Dark and light themes — toggle between themes to suit your preference
    • No server required — runs entirely in the browser, your data never leaves your device

    How to Use

    Paste a timestamp into the input field or type a relative expression like "2 hours ago". The converter auto-detects the format and displays the equivalent value in Unix (seconds and milliseconds), ISO 8601, RFC 2822, SQL datetime, relative time, and human-readable formats. Use the timezone dropdown to see the converted time in any timezone. Click "Now" to convert the current time, or "Clear" to reset. Click any "Copy" button to copy a value to your clipboard.
